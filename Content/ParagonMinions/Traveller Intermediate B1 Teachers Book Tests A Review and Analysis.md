## What is Traveller Intermediate B1?
 
Traveller Intermediate B1 is part of the Traveller series by MM Publications, which comes in two editions: British English (BrE) and American English (AmE). The course features motivating and contemporary topics with multicultural and cross-curricular information, lively dialogues presenting real spoken English, an integrated approach to the development of the four skills, special emphasis on vocabulary building, grammar presented and practised in context, systematic development of reading and listening skills and subskills, a variety of communicative tasks, a step-by-step approach to writing, activities encouraging critical thinking, practical tips helping students to become autonomous learners, a round-up section in each module providing regular revision and consolidation, a grammar reference section, songs, tapescripts, and a DVD[^1^].
 
## What does the teacher's book offer?
 
The teacher's book for Traveller Intermediate B1 provides a comprehensive guide for teachers on how to use the course effectively. It includes:
 
- An introduction with information on the course philosophy, methodology, components, and CEFR correlation.
- A detailed lesson plan for each unit of the student's book, with clear objectives, warm-up activities, answers to exercises, additional notes and tips, suggestions for extra activities and homework assignments.
- A workbook teacher's edition with overprinted answers.
- A teacher's resource CD/CD-ROM with photocopiable worksheets for grammar, vocabulary, communication, writing, songs, tests (progress tests, module tests, term tests), answer keys, audio scripts.
- A class CD set with all the recordings for the student's book.
- A DVD with video clips related to the topics of the student's book.
- A DVD activity book with tasks to accompany the video clips.
- An interactive whiteboard (IWB) material with interactive versions of the student's book pages and additional activities.

## What do the tests consist of?
 
The tests for Traveller Intermediate B1 are designed to assess the students' progress and achievement in relation to the CEFR levels. They include:

- Progress tests: one for each module of the student's book, covering grammar, vocabulary, reading comprehension, listening comprehension, writing.
- Module tests: one for every two modules of the student's book, covering grammar, vocabulary, reading comprehension, listening comprehension.
- Term tests: one for every four modules of the student's book, covering grammar, vocabulary, reading comprehension, listening comprehension.

The tests are available in two formats: paper-based or digital. The digital format allows teachers to administer the tests online or offline using a computer or a tablet. The digital format also provides instant feedback and scores for the students and generates reports for the teachers[^3^].
